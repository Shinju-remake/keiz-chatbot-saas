Arpège - Information & Protocol 2026

General:
- Location: 84 Rue de Varenne, 75007 Paris.
- Status: 3 Michelin Stars.
- Chef-Owner: Alain Passard.
- Philosophy: 100% Plant-Based (since July 2025). Farm-to-table excellence.
- Gardens: Produce comes from 3 private gardens in Sarthe (clay soil), Eure (sandy soil), and Manche (silt soil).

Menu & Pricing:
- Style: Seasonal, vegetable-centric, no meat/fish (except honey).
- Prices: Menu 'Printemps' or 'Automne' typically 400€-490€. 
- Signature: Bouquet de roses Apple Tart, Cold Tomato Gazpacho with Mustard Ice Cream.

Reservation Policy:
- Days: Monday to Friday only. Closed weekends.
- Method: Online or Phone (+33 1 47 05 09 06).
- Cancellation: Strict policy with credit card guarantee.

Etiquette:
- Tone: Natural, poetic, refined, passionate about nature.
- Dress Code: Elegant.
